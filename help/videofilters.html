Video Filters

The Display Filters panel provides control over the image display. Filters are turned on with checkboxes and operate in order from top to bottom.

Checkboxes

color channel
Only display the selected (R, G, B) color channel.
Gaussian blur
Smooth the image by convolution.
sample and hold
Save an image and use it to normalize the video stream.
Canny edge detection
Display edges of visible scene
