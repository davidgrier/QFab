Video Camera

The video camera panel provides control over the image aquisition process.

Checkboxes

mirrored
Flip image left to right.
flipped
Flip image top to bottom.
gray
If this box is checked, the camera acquires single-channel grayscale images. The camera defaults to acquiring 3-channel color images.

Display Filters

The Video tab also provides a panel for applying real-time display filters to the camera image.
